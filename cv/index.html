Currently a Senior Data Analyst on the Business Analytics team at Blue Cross Blue Shield of Massachusetts.

My work focuses mainly on developing models and designing studys to measure the impact of programs that support our Commerical and Medicare members. I work closely with business partners across the organzation to rapidly iterate through R&D discovery phase and in turn build out reproducible and scaleable results. I also support with the the training and development, integrating new tools and building out a new workflow for analytics within my team.

Prior to my work at Blue Cross, while completing my master's degree I was a Data Analytics Fellow on the City of Boston's Citywide Analytics team. I focused on peformance measurement where I worked with a few departments helping to improve their consumption of analytics by recommending KPI's and building out dashboards for real-time measurement.

Before this life, coming out of undergrad, I served 2-years as an AmeriCorps Member with City Year––an organization that uses data and small group interventions to improve outcomes of low performing high-need urban schools. The school I worked with went through an impressive transition over the two years, and at the end of the 2014-2015 school year was the school was the most improved K-8 school in Boston in ELA and Math. Furthermore, they ranked #2 and #4 in most improved K-8 in ELA and Math, respectively, across all of Massachusetts. I took that experience with me to the Achievement Network, an education consulting organzation, and focused on our teacher facing web platform as well as supported our consultants with a data-driven approach to resource selection and curation based on school and district needs.

I have a Bachlor's of Business Administration from University of Massachusetts Amherst and a Master's in Analytics from Bentley University.
